A little about us

Founded in 2021, Eagles, Inc is a leading IT services firm, with customers in the private and government sectors from some of Philippines' largest brands and household names. Our services from start to finish are provided and executed 100% locally by our professional, experienced and qualified team. We have constantly evolved with these shifts, utilizing our experience and expertise to provide the highest quality service to our valuable customers. We pride ourselves on our high level of professionalism and integrity in every aspect of our services. Our ultimate goal is to create a company that is solely built on trust and honesty, understanding the IT needs of our clients.

The Executive Director, Mr. Kevin Dykes De Vera Aguila, realized there was a better way to provide ICT solutions that could allow him to ''Do More for Less'' in the process. Better yet, it involved improving uptime while reducing the costs. Since he founded Eagles, Inc. in 2021, Kevin has been its Director responsible for its overall strategic direction, its consulting services and its business development
